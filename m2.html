Tori Paitan Ramen

Unlike traditional clear broths, this "white soup" is created by slow-simmering chicken bone broth for hours until it reaches a thick, milky consistency packed with natural collagen. Served with chewy wheat noodles and topped with tender halal chicken slices, this bowl offers a deeply satisfying, savory umami experience that is both hearty and refined.

View Ingredients
Slow-simmered chicken bone broth, artisanal wheat noodles, halal-certified soy sauce, sea salt, tender chicken slices, green onions, bamboo shoots (menma), wood ear mushrooms, and nori seaweed.
$15.99 Serves 1-2

Ebi Tempura

We take succulent, jumbo shrimp and coat them in a delicate, ice-cold tempura batter before flash-frying them to a pale, golden crisp. This method ensures the shrimp remains tender and sweet on the inside while the outside stays airy and light, providing a satisfying shatter with every bite.

Fresh jumbo shrimp, tempura wheat flour, ice-cold filtered water, cornstarch, vegetable frying oil, sea salt, and a side of halal-certified dipping sauce (tentsuyu).

Hojicha

Unlike traditional green teas, the roasting process transforms the leaves into a deep reddish-brown color, replacing the usual grassy notes with a toasty, nutty flavor and hints of caramel. Naturally lower in caffeine and acidity, it offers a gentle, smoky finish that makes it an exceptionally calming beverage to enjoy at any time of day.

View Ingredients
Roasted Japanese green tea leaves (Bancha or Sencha) and hot filtered water.
$4.99 Serves 1
